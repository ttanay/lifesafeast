Tangerine and Carrot Cooler

  1. 2 pound carrots, peeled and cut into dices
  2. 2 tablespoons honey
  3. 1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg
  4. 1/2 gallon fresh tangerine juice
  5. 1 cup ice cubes
  6. 1 orange, cut into 6 slices for garnish, cut from one side

In a blender, blend together all the ingredients. Pass the puree through a fi ne mesh sieve placed over a bowl. Serve chilled in a tall glass garnished with fresh orange.
