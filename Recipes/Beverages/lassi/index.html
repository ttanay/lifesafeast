Salted Lassi with Mint and Toasted Cumin

  1. 4 cups plain low-fat yogurt
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 1 cup crushed ice
  4. Salt to taste
  5. 1/2 teaspoon toasted cumin seeds
  6. 6 fresh mint leaves

In a blender add the yogurt, water, ice, and salt and blend until frothy. Serve chilled garnished with mint leaves and cumin seeds.
