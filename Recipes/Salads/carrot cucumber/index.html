Carrot and Cucumber Salad

  1. 4 tablespoons plain yogurt
  2. 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted and ground
  3. 2 teaspoon cumin seeds, toasted and ground
  4. 1/2 pound cucumbers, peeled seeded and diced
  5. 1/2 pound carrots, grated
  6. Salt to taste
  7. 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  8. 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  9. 1/2 teaspoon nigella seeds
  10. 1 green cayenne chile, slit lengthwise and seeded
  11. 1/4 easpoon cayenne
  12. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  13. 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  14. 2 tablespoons mint leaves, minced

In a small bowl add the yogurt, sesame and cumin and mix well to make a smooth paste. Add the cucumber, carrot and salt. Set aside. Heat the oil in as small skillet over medium heat. Add the mustard, nigella, and chile and cook for about a minute, stirring until the spices are aromatic. Add the cayenne and turmeric Stir and pour the fl avored oil over the cucumbers; toss gently. Add the lemon juice and chill.
