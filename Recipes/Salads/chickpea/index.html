Chickpea Salad with Tamarind dressing and fresh Cilantro

  1. 15 ounces chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  2. 1 medium red onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  4. salt to taste
  5. 4 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Mix all the ingredients in a large (non-reactive) mixing bowl and serve cold as a salad or a side dish.
