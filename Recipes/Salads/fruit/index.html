Fresh Fruit Salad with Mint Dressing

  1. 2 cups mixed seasonal fruits
  2. Juice of 1 lemon
  3. Salt to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  6. 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint

Mix all the ingredients until well combined. Keep refrigerated and serve chilled when required. and Serve cold topped with mint leaves
