Mashed Potatoes with Cumin flavored Patties

  1. 1 pound russet or red boiling potatoes, peeled and boiled
  2. 1/4 cup All purpose flour
  3. 1 cup semolina
  4. 2 Green chilies such as serrano, seeded and finely chopped
  5. 1 medium Spanish onion, finely chopped
  6. 1 teaspoon toasted cumin powder
  7. 1 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  8. 1/2 cup Cilantro
  9. Salt to taste (about 1 teaspoon, usually)
  10. Vegetable oil for frying

In a large mixing bowl mash the potatoes and add all the remaining ingredients. Shape them into small patties, cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes. Heat the oil to 350 F. Fry the patties until golden brown and cooked through. Serve hot with your favourite chutney.
